Privacy Policy

We do not collect, use, save or have access to any of your personal data when your are interacting with SL Commuter.

Individual settings relating to SL Commuter are not personal and are stored only on your device(s).

The traffic data on the App is retrieved from APIs by Trafiklab.se. You can read more about how they process personal data at their website.

To help us better understand how people use our apps and to continue improving them, we use analytics information provided by App Store Connect. This service provide us with some generic usage, transaction, and other statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.
